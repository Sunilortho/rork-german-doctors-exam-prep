## ðŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### "Something went wrong"
- Try **Method 2** (scan QR code instead)
- Make sure you have **Expo Go** app installed
- Check your internet connection
- Restart Expo Go app

### Microphone not working
- Grant microphone permission when prompted
- On iOS: Settings > Expo Go > Microphone > Allow
- On Android: Settings > Apps > Expo Go > Permissions > Microphone

### Blank screen
- Force close Expo Go app
- Reinstall Expo Go
- Try a different access method
